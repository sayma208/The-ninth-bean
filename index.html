About coffee

At The Ninth Bean, coffee isn’t just a beverage

it’s a story that begins long before the first sip. Every bean carries a journey — from the lush

sun-kissed farms where it’s grown, to the careful hands that roast it to perfection, and finally, to the cup that warms your heart

Our name, The Ninth Bean, reflects our philosophy — every eighth bean may be ordinary, but the ninth is special

It’s the one that stands out with a deeper aroma, a richer flavor, and a touch of magic. That’s what we aim to serve in every cup — something that feels familiar yet unforgettable

We source our beans from some of the finest coffee-growing regions, ensuring they are ethically grown,

Market Reasearch About Our Ninth Bean

Before creating The Ninth Bean, we set out to understand what today’s café lovers truly want.

Coffee culture has evolved — it’s no longer just about grabbing a cup on the go.

People now look for experiences — cozy spaces, aesthetic interiors, comfort food, and drinks that speak to their mood.

Our market research revealed a strong demand for premium yet affordable cafés that combine great taste, relaxed ambiance,

and Instagram-worthy presentation. Consumers, especially young adults and working professionals, are seeking cafés where they can work, connect, or unwind

all in one place.
